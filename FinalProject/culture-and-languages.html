Nigeria Culture and Diversity

There are over 500 languages spoken in Nigeria. However, the official language of the country is English, which serves as the lingua franca for communication across different about 250 ethnic groups. There are approximately 250 ethnic groups or tribes in Nigeria. These ethnic groups are diverse in terms of culture, language, traditions, and customs, contributing to the rich cultural heritage of the country. Some of the major tribes include Hausa, Yoruba, Igbo, Fulani, Ijaw, Kanuri, Tiv, and many more.
